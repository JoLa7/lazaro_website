Leveraging Large Language Models for Adaptive Mental Health Training

AI-powered chat copilots are emerging as valuable tools to support less-experienced counselors and expand care in underserved regions. However, they risk fostering overreliance and acceptance of flawed suggestions, potentially leading to harmful crisis responses. Partnering with an India-based crisis hotline, our team developed and benchmarked synthetic LLM-driven patient agents and deployed an AI copilot in live clinical workflows, enabling evaluation of new training paradigms with expert counselor feedback for real-time improvement.

ML Probabilistic Weather Forecasting

We developed a probabilistic temperature forecasting framework to improve West Nile Virus risk assessments in California. Using historical climate data and machine learning models, including XGBoost Distribution, Bayesian neural networks, and Gaussian Processes, we generated full probability distributions of monthly temperatures using Stochastic & Monte Carlo Simulations, enabling uncertainty quantification.
